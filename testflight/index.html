TestFlight Testing Addendum

Last updated: November 18, 2025

This Addendum applies when you install a TestFlight or other pre-release version of PicSeal (“Pre-Release Software”). It supplements the PicSeal Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

By using a TestFlight build, you agree to this Addendum.

1. Experimental nature

Pre-Release Software may:

  • Be incomplete or unstable.
  • Contain bugs or performance issues.
  • Change significantly before public release.
  • Be removed at any time.

Do not rely on Pre-Release Software for preserving or storing important photos.

2. Additional data collection during testing

In addition to the data described in the Privacy Policy, TestFlight builds may collect:

  • Additional error logs.
  • Additional performance or failure data.
  • Diagnostic information needed to evaluate scanning and enhancement features.

We do not collect screen recordings or session replays.

3. Scanner training data (optional)

If you enable Help improve PicSeal, scanner photos you capture in TestFlight builds may be uploaded to help us:

  • Diagnose scanning issues.
  • Test variations of detection or cropping algorithms.
  • Evaluate new feature concepts.
  • Improve training datasets and model accuracy.

Turning the toggle off stops new uploads but does not remove photos already used for development or model improvement.

4. Data resets

During testing, we may:

  • Reset accounts.
  • Clear test data.
  • Change backend structures.
  • Modify or delete information stored by the test environment.

Always keep personal copies of important photos outside PicSeal.

5. Feedback

If you submit feedback, you grant us a license to use that feedback to improve the Service.
